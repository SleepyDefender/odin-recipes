Coconut Bake

Description

Roti is roti! a flatbread that not only fills the stomach but the soul, soft, tasty and when eated with channa and aloo, nothing better

  • 9 Cups Flour
  • 1 lb split peas (yellow lentils I think)
  • 8 oz Butter
  • seasoning, whole heap
  • Salt

Steps

  1. In a large bowl combine flour, butter, salt
  2. Add water as needed, knead until its no longer sticky but NOT firm
  3. Grind cooked split peas, add seasonings, taste, add what you like
  4. Stuff dough with split peas mixture. cook on tawah till no longer raw :D
