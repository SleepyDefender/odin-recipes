Coconut Bake

Description

Coconut bake has been a staple in the Caribbean as a filling and delicious breakfast. usually peered with saltfish, cheese, smoked herring and many other foods.

  • 9 Cups Flour
  • 2 tbs Cinnamon
  • 8 oz Butter
  • 2 packs of yeast
  • nutmeg powder
  • 2 cups coconut cream

Steps

  1. In a large bowl combine flour, butter, cinnamon and yeast
  2. Add the coconut cream to the dry mixture
  3. knead dough, add water as needed
  4. when dough is mixed and soft bake for 20-30 minutes at the temperature of your choosing
